1.
二月春风，柳色初青。每到这个时节，长安以东的大片郊野便会被一大片碧色所沁染，一条条绿绦在官道两旁依依垂下，积枝成行，有若十里步障。唯有灞桥附近，是个例外。
In February's vernal breeze, the willows first don their green hue. Every year at this season, the vast eastern outskirts of Chang'an become imbued with emerald shades, as rows of jade pendants cascade along the official roads, their intertwined branches forming continuous green corridors like ten-mile ceremonial screens. Only near Ba Bridge does this pattern break.
2.

3.
李善德出城的时候，既没折柳，也没买枝，他没那心情。唯一陪伴自己上路的，只有一头高大的河套骏马，以及一条鼓鼓囊囊的马搭子。
李善德离城时，既未折柳赠别，也未购花留念——他全无这般闲情。唯一伴他踏上旅程的，只有一匹高大的河套骏马，和一条塞得鼓鼓囊囊的马褡裢。
4.

On the day he resolved to journey to Lingnan, Han Cheng personally imparted some instructions to him. The following day, Li Shande visited the Shanglin Office again, shedding his usual submissive demeanor. He demanded that Administrator Liu prepare thirty strings of coins for the courier fees and travel expenses to Lingnan.
5.

刘署令勃然大怒，呵斥道你既是荔枝使，要么去开圣人的内帑大盈库支取，要么向户部度支司郎中讨要，与我这上林署何干？李善德却亮出敕牒，指着"奉敕佥荐李善德监事勾当本事"那行字，说这"佥荐"二字既由您亲笔签署，自然该先从上林署调拨钱粮，再由上林署向度支司申报核销。

8.

After leaving the Shanglin Bureau, Li Shande proceeded to the Treasury of Tallies and Seals, where he obtained a postal relay voucher in his capacity as the Lychee Envoy. With this document, he could lodge at any courier station along the official routes free of charge, with both provisions for himself and fodder for his horse covered by the imperial court.
9.

既然沿途驿站已提供食宿保障，上林署发放的"驿使钱"与"出食钱"实则形同虚设。使职制度的精妙之处正在于此——它跳脱出各部门的运作流程：符宝司不会与上林署核对账目，上林署亦无法向户部查证款项虚实，三套系统彼此隔绝互不相通。
10.

11.

On February 5th, Li Shande crossed the Ba Bridge, left Chang'an, and without hesitation raced swiftly eastward.
