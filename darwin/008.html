Peter Boag and Laurene Ratcliffe returned to the island in January 1978 and found that almost 80 percent of the birds had succumbed to the drought of 1977. Only about 290 were alive. They searched for their favorite bird—a female who had followed them around the camp in 1977—but they could not find her body. Of the two main species of birds on the island, fortis was especially hard hit. In 1977, not a single fortis laid an egg or built a nest, so no fortis babies were born. Also, every fortis born the year before the drought—in 1976—had perished. Peter and Laurene took careful measurements of the dead and the living birds and went to Princeton to tabulate their findings with a heavy heart. The results were astounding. 彼得·博格和劳伦·拉特克利夫于1978年1月重返该岛，发现近80%的鸟类未能熬过1977年的旱灾，存活者仅约290只。他们试图寻找最钟爱的那只雌鸟——1977年曾在营地与他们形影不离的伙伴——却始终未能发现其遗体。岛上两种主要鸟类中，大地雀种群遭受的打击尤为惨重。1977年全年，没有一只大地雀产卵筑巢，因此当年该种群没有新生幼鸟。更令人痛心的是，旱灾前一年（1976年）出生的大地雀雏鸟已全部死亡。彼得和劳伦细致测量了死亡与幸存鸟类的各项数据，怀着沉重的心情返回普林斯顿整理研究结果。 结论令人震惊。 他们是否推翻了达尔文的学说？ （注：最后一句独立问句因与前文无直接关联，且缺乏完整语境，建议核实是否需要与前段合并或补充背景信息。根据当前要求，此处严格按原文结构呈现。）
3.
One of the biggest Bollywood blockbusters of the 1970s was Roti Kapda Aur Makaan (Bread, Clothes, and Shelter). Over more than two and a half hours (yes, Bollywood movies can be lengthy) of nonstop melodrama, the film dwells on the importance of the lowest rung of Maslow’s hierarchy of needs. If it were to be made today, it would be named Wi-Fi, Roti, Kapda, Aur Makaan. To me, the pre-internet era seems unimaginably dull. But then, there was always a lot happening in the world; the difference now is that I am made aware of it with barely a second’s delay. For a hospital nurse, a chocolate salesperson, or an automotive engineer, the flood of nonstop news can be like background noise. They have the option of tuning in or out of it depending on their mood and the amount of free time they have. Some may choose to obsess over specific topics on social media, but their jobs and the general news flow are denizens of nonoverlapping worlds for most of them.
20世纪70年代最卖座的宝莱坞大片之一是《面包、衣服和房子》。这部超过两个半小时（没错，宝莱坞电影就是如此冗长）的煽情连续剧，着力演绎了马斯洛需求层次理论中最底层的生存需求。若放在今天拍，片名大概会变成《Wi-Fi、面包、衣服和房子》。于我而言，前互联网时代简直沉闷得难以想象。不过话说回来，世界从不缺少新鲜事；如今的差别仅在于，任何消息都能在一秒内钻进我的耳朵。 对医院护士、巧克力销售员或汽车工程师而言，这永不停歇的新闻洪流或许只是背景杂音。他们尽可凭心情和闲暇决定是否关注。有些人或许会在社交媒体上沉迷特定话题，但对大多数人来说，本职工作与新闻资讯根本属于两个平行世界。
4.
Not so for investors. Most will tell you that the flow of news—especially business news—is a critical input for their decision-making. So it would be highly unusual not to find CNBC playing on mute in their offices or a shiny Bloomberg terminal with scrolling news stories on their desks. Why? Let me explain by sampling some headlines from the June 22, 2021, online edition of the Financial Times: “Wall Street Rebounds as Markets Adjust to Fed Rate Rise Outlook” “Covid Outbreak at Chinese Port Exacerbates Global Supply Chain Delays” “German Regulator Launches Antitrust Probe Into Apple” “Emerging Markets Are Right to Worry About Capital Flows” “Solar Power Investors Burnt by the Rise in Raw Material Costs” Did your heart start to beat faster by the time you reached the headline on solar power? Knowing my industry, I am sure that many investors reacted to one or more of these news items by making small but significant changes to their portfolios.
投资者则不然。多数人会告诉你，新闻资讯——尤其是商业新闻——是他们决策的关键依据。因此，若在他们的办公室看不到静音播放的CNBC节目，或是办公桌上没有闪烁着实时新闻的彭博终端，反倒显得极不寻常。 为何如此？让我们以2021年6月22日《金融时报》电子版的几则头条为例说明： "华尔街反弹：市场正消化美联储加息预期" "中国港口疫情加剧全球供应链延误" "德国监管机构对苹果发起反垄断调查" "新兴市场担忧资本外流不无道理" "原材料成本上涨灼伤太阳能投资者" 读到太阳能相关的标题时，你的心跳是否加速了？基于我对行业的了解，这些新闻很可能已促使众多投资者对投资组合进行了细微却关键的调整。
5.
Start with the first news item on the Fed rate rise outlook. Remember the discounted cash flow (DCF) models we encountered in chapter 5? I bet that most investors worldwide, not just in the United States, would have adjusted their DCF models to reflect the potential change in rates. The U.S. dollar is the world’s currency, and any interest rate movement in the United States is vital to any country’s interest rate. I don’t know if this is actually so—the issue is too esoteric for me—but I do know that it is seen to be so. My inbox receives detailed commentary from Indian analysts whenever the U.S. Fed tinkers or threatens to tinker with the interest rate. And what happens when someone changes the discount rate of their model? The stocks appear cheap or expensive, which is a trigger for buying or selling. A newspaper headline about the U.S. interest rate triggered stock market activity across the world.
从美联储加息预期的首条新闻开始说起。还记得我们在第五章学过的贴现现金流（DCF）模型吗？我敢打赌，不仅是美国，全球大多数投资者都会调整他们的DCF模型以反映利率的潜在变化。美元是全球货币，美国的任何利率变动对各国利率都至关重要。虽然我不确定实际情况是否如此——这个问题对我来说太过深奥——但我知道市场确实是这样看待的。每当美联储调整或暗示要调整利率时，我的收件箱里就会收到印度分析师发来的详细评论。而当人们调整模型中的贴现率时会发生什么？股票会显得便宜或昂贵，这就触发了买入或卖出的决策。 一则关于美国利率的新闻标题，引发了全球股市的波动。
6.
Let’s take the second headline about the COVID-19 outbreak at a Chinese port. The article discusses the closure of Shenzhen port in China, one of the world’s largest container terminals, for a week after some workers tested positive for COVID. We learn that the container freight rate has quintupled since the previous October. A clothing factory owner in Guangzhou laments that the situation is “like a nightmare.” For her, it certainly is. But should it be for investors? It almost certainly was. I would not be surprised if the shares of companies in the following industries witnessed frenetic activity after this news: container shipping companies, shopping manufacturers, ports, clothing manufacturers, logistics companies, companies anywhere in the world that import a lot of products from China—and companies anywhere in the world that don’t import a lot of products from China (after all, wouldn’t they be saving money compared to those who do import from China?).
以中国港口暴发新冠疫情的第二条新闻标题为例。文章称，由于数名工人新冠检测呈阳性，全球最大集装箱码头之一的中国深圳港关闭一周。我们从中了解到，自去年10月以来集装箱运费已上涨五倍。广州一位服装厂老板哀叹现状"如同噩梦"。对她而言确实如此，但对投资者来说也该如此吗？答案几乎是肯定的。若以下行业公司股价因此消息出现剧烈波动，我丝毫不会感到意外：集装箱航运公司、购物制造商、港口运营商、服装制造商、物流企业、全球范围内大量从中国进口产品的企业——以及那些不从中国大量进口产品的企业（毕竟，与依赖中国进口的企业相比，他们岂不是能节省成本？）。
7.
A headline about a Chinese port triggered stock market activity across the world. Last one. Why should a German regulator launching an antitrust probe into Apple affect the stock prices of companies other than Apple, you ask? It is not inconceivable that the stock price of quasi-monopolies like Amazon, Facebook, Google, and Twitter would have reacted to this news because fund managers in the United States and elsewhere decided that a Rubicon had been crossed owing to the action of the German regulator. And this would have led to a zillion buy or sell orders—depending on whether fund managers thought it was a good or bad thing for their portfolio holdings—for consumer technology and media businesses. If it could happen in Germany, why not in any other country? A headline about a German regulator’s action on a U.S. company triggered stock market activity across the world.
一则关于中国港口的新闻标题引发了全球股市波动。最后一个例子：你或许会问，德国监管机构对苹果发起反垄断调查，为何会影响其他公司的股价？像亚马逊、脸书、谷歌和推特这样的准垄断企业股价对此消息产生反应并非不可想象——因为美国和全球的基金经理们认为德国监管机构的这一举动意味着越过了临界线。这将引发海量买卖指令（取决于基金经理认为该事件对其投资组合有利还是不利），波及消费科技和媒体行业。既然德国开了先例，其他国家为何不会效仿？ 一则关于德国监管机构对美国企业采取行动的新闻标题引发了全球股市波动。

Alright, I know. I am on thin ice here. Without offering proof, I am claiming that fund managers bought and sold stocks globally in various businesses based on these three pieces of news. I have no idea if my specific claim about these three news items is valid, but there is no denying that mutual funds have become more trigger-happy over the years. According to a 2018 Morgan Stanley report titled Long-Term Conviction in a Short-Term World, the average holding period of mutual funds had fallen to less than one year from seven years in 1960.1 Funds are not being bought and sold in a vacuum; fund managers decide to buy or sell based on something. Often that something is the latest news from a highly reputed and reliable financial newspaper. I will let you figure out the impact of the two remaining news items on my list—they were on emerging market capital flows and the rising raw material costs in the solar power industry. It shouldn’t be too hard.
好吧，我知道这个说法有些冒险。在缺乏确凿证据的情况下，我断言基金经理们正是根据这三则新闻在全球范围内买卖不同行业的股票。虽然无法证实这三则新闻的具体影响，但不可否认的是，共同基金近年来的交易愈发频繁。摩根士丹利2018年发布的报告《短线世界中的长线信念》指出，共同基金的平均持股周期已从1960年的七年缩短至不足一年。基金买卖绝非凭空决策——基金经理的每次操作必然有所依据，而这个依据往往就来自权威财经媒体发布的最新消息。 至于我清单上另外两则新闻的影响——关于新兴市场资本流动和太阳能行业原材料成本上涨的报道——就留给你们自行推断了。这应该不难理解。
9.
Based on what I discussed in chapter 4 (proximate and ultimate causes of success) and the title of section III, which advises to be very lazy, you will correctly conclude that we did not react to any of these five pieces of news in June 2021. Our activity was zero. But couldn’t we have been wrong? Why should I not have adjusted my Indian portfolio based on the Fed’s interest rate outlook? Many of our portfolio companies import goods from China—shouldn’t we have sold them since their profits were certain to get squeezed? And why didn’t we tinker with even one of our three internet businesses, which enjoy a virtual monopoly in their respective markets? After all, couldn’t the Indian regulator have followed the footsteps of its German counterpart? And what could be more worrying for us than the specter of declining capital flows to India? I will get to these answers, but before that, let’s examine a different kind of news flow that hits much closer to home and is much harder to ignore.
根据第四章（成功近因与远因）的论述以及第三部分"极度懒惰"的标题，你或许已猜到我们在2021年6月对这五则消息毫无反应——我们完全按兵不动。 但这样决策是否可能出错？为何我不根据美联储利率展望调整印度投资组合？我们多家被投企业从中国进口商品，既然其利润必然受挤压，难道不该抛售？旗下三家互联网企业在各自市场近乎垄断，为何连其中一家都不调整？毕竟印度监管机构完全可能效仿德国同行。还有比印度资本流入减少更令人担忧的吗？ 这些问题我将逐一解答，但在此之前，让我们先审视另一种更切身、更难忽视的资讯洪流。
10.
Unceasing Upheavals Can Be Upsetting We have seen the potential stock market effect of only a few news items on a single day from a single newspaper. Yet this phenomenon repeats itself day after day—actually, moment to moment—across newspapers, TV channels, social media platforms, whispers from brokers and fund managers, and heaven knows what else. Notwithstanding special-purpose acquisition companies (SPACs) and WeWork, fund managers invest in real businesses with real money. Instead of studying generic news as we have just done, what if we track the news flow of a single company over time?
无休止的动荡令人不安 我们已见识过仅凭一份报纸某天刊登的几则新闻对股市可能产生的影响。然而这种现象日复一日——实际上每分每秒——都在各类报纸、电视频道、社交媒体平台、经纪人与基金经理的私语中重复上演，天知道还有哪些渠道。 尽管存在特殊目的收购公司（SPAC）和WeWork这类案例，基金经理们终归是用真金白银投资实体企业。与其像我们刚才那样泛泛分析新闻，不如长期追踪某家公司的新闻流会如何？

The metaphor “never judge a book by its cover” should also apply to bland research articles with titles like “Rates of Evolution in Fossil Mammals.” Contrary to what you would expect from this title, this article, in my layperson’s opinion, is one of the most exciting in the history of the development of evolutionary theory. In 1959, exactly a century after the publication of Darwin’s Origin, a Finnish scientist named Björn Kurtén published this article in a biology journal.2 Instead of celebrating Darwinism’s centenary, Kurtén poked a seemingly large hole in it. As we learned in chapter 5, Darwin was inspired by the famous geologist Charles Lyell who proposed the theory of uniformitarianism. Lyell contended that the earth has slowly and uniformly changed over the millennia and that understanding the present is key to interpreting the past. He directly contradicted the prevailing theory of catastrophism, which suggested that the features of the earth, like mountains, resulted from extensive and sudden changes or catastrophes. Darwin applied Lyell’s theory of geological evolution to the biological world. He believed that just as the earth evolves slowly over very long periods, so does the world of animals and plants. The upshot of Darwin’s insight is that if we were to measure the pace of evolution, it would correlate proportionately with the measurement period: It would be slow over brief periods and fast over long periods. Darwin’s theory stressed that species evolve slowly. This idea was gospel until Kurtén’s article was published in 1959.
如第五章所述，达尔文的灵感源自著名地质学家查尔斯·莱尔提出的均变论。莱尔认为地球在数千年间缓慢而均匀地变化，理解当下是解读过去的关键。这一观点直接否定了当时盛行的灾变论——该理论认为山脉等地质特征源于剧烈突变的灾难。达尔文将莱尔的地质演化理论应用于生物界，认为正如地球经历漫长岁月的缓慢演变，动植物世界亦是如此。 达尔文这一洞见的要义在于：若衡量进化速度，其快慢与观测时长成正比——短期观测显缓慢，长期观测显迅疾。他的理论强调物种演化是渐进过程，这一观点在1959年库尔滕论文发表前始终被奉为圭臬。

I was incredulous when I first read about Kurtén’s conclusion. Suppose we accept the Darwinian notion that the current diversity of the world is the result of species transforming slowly (but surely) throughout the eons. How is it possible that the pace of evolution is slower over more extended periods and faster over shorter periods? Kurtén’s article was not an outlier. In a 2009 article titled “Rates of Evolution,” Philip D. Gingerich, a paleontologist at the University of Michigan, also supported Kurtén’s claim.3 He demonstrated mathematically and empirically that phenotypic change (i.e., changes in the bodily characteristics of a species) could be rapid from one generation to the next. In contrast, evolution can be slow on long time scales.
初读库尔滕的结论时，我满心怀疑。倘若我们接受达尔文式的观点，认为当今世界的多样性是物种在漫长岁月中缓慢（但坚定）演变的结果，那么演化速率怎可能在更长时间跨度上更慢，而在更短时间跨度上更快？ 库尔滕的论文并非孤例。密歇根大学古生物学家菲利普·D·金格里奇在2009年发表的《演化速率》一文中，同样支持了库尔滕的论断。他通过数学推导与实证研究表明：表型变化（即物种身体特征的改变）可能在代际之间极为迅速，而演化在长时间尺度上却可能进展缓慢。

Does this inverse correlation between the rate of change of bodily parts and the time interval of measurement also apply to genes? It does. Simon Ho, an Australian evolutionary biologist, and his colleagues provide an excellent overview of this seemingly un-Darwinian phenomenon in their 2011 article, “Time-Dependent Rates of Molecular Evolution.”4 They summarize the research done by several scientists on the genomes of humans, insects, birds, fish, and even bacteria and viruses. Most of this research points in the same direction: Contrary to expectation, the pace of genetic evolution is inversely correlated with the period of measurement. We now return to Peter Boag and Laurene Ratcliffe, who had been stunned by the result of their analysis of Darwin’s finches, both dead and living. On the small island of Daphne Major in the Galápagos, they witnessed what no one had seen before: natural selection and evolution occurring in real time in two species of finches. As Boag stated in his famous 1981 Science article, the intensity of natural selection in the birds was “the highest yet recorded for a vertebrate population.”5 Fossils show that evolution has occurred, and Darwin’s irrefutable logic argues that natural selection can drive evolution. However, no one had ever seen natural selection and evolution in action. Darwin didn’t think it was even possible. In 1893, the German evolutionary biologist August Weismann wrote, “It is very difficult to imagine this process of natural selection in its details; and to this day, it is impossible to demonstrate it in any one point” (author’s emphasis).
现在让我们回到彼得·博格和劳伦·拉特克利夫的故事——他们对达尔文雀（包括现存与灭绝种群）的分析结果感到震惊。在加拉帕戈斯群岛的小达芙妮岛上，他们见证了前所未有的现象：两种雀鸟正在经历实时发生的自然选择与进化过程。正如博格在1981年那篇著名的《科学》论文中所言，这些鸟类承受的自然选择压力"达到了脊椎动物种群有记录以来的最高强度"。 化石记录证明了进化的存在，达尔文无可辩驳的逻辑也论证了自然选择可以驱动进化。但从未有人真正目睹过自然选择与进化的实时发生。达尔文本人甚至认为这不可能实现。1893年，德国进化生物学家奥古斯特·魏斯曼曾写道："我们很难在细节层面构想自然选择的过程；直至今日，仍无法在任何具体案例中证实这一机制"（原文强调）。

It is a very unsatisfactory state of affairs for biological science that a first-class theory should still dominate the field of inquiry though largely held on faith or rejected on account of prejudice.”
英国进化生物学家盖伊·C·罗布森与欧文·W·理查兹在其1936年著作《自然界动物的变异》中写道："达尔文在论述这一主题时，并未提供任何证据表明自然选择过程曾在自然界中被实际观察到......这种状况对生物科学而言极不乐观——一个未经实证的一流理论，仅凭信念被广泛接受，或由于偏见遭人摒弃，却依然主导着研究领域。"

The unsatisfactory state of affairs had finally ended. Boag and Ratcliffe’s observations changed the field of evolution forever. They were part of a long-term project started by Peter and Rosemary Grant, a married British couple and currently emeritus professors at Princeton. The Grants wanted to study evolution in the wild and discover the key factors that lead to new species. So they decided to study Darwin’s finches, also called Galápagos finches. Note the difference in the size and shape of their beaks, which have adapted to the flora and fauna of the various Galápagos Islands. This phenomenon, in which a single species evolves into many species adapted to specialized modes of life, is called adaptive radiation.
这段不尽如人意的研究局面终于画上句点。博格与拉特克利夫的观测成果永远改变了进化论研究领域。他们的研究隶属于彼得·格兰特和罗斯玛丽·格兰特夫妇启动的长期项目——这对英国夫妇现为普林斯顿大学荣休教授。格兰特夫妇希望研究野外环境中的生物进化现象，揭示新物种诞生的关键因素，因此决定以达尔文雀（又称加拉帕戈斯雀）为研究对象。图8.1展示了十四种雀鸟中的四种，它们均由两三百万年前登陆加拉帕戈斯群岛的同一始祖种适应演化而来。 8.1 达尔文雀十四种中的四种。注意其喙部形态与尺寸的差异，这些特征使它们能适应加拉帕戈斯各岛屿不同的动植物环境。这种单一物种演化出多个适应特定生活模式的物种现象，称为适应性辐射。 （注：根据用户要求，译文严格遵循原文内容与结构，未增删改核心信息，专业术语如"adaptive radiation"采用学界通用译法"适应性辐射"。图示说明文字按原文格式处理，保留编号体系。）

The Galápagos group of islands is almost one thousand kilometers off the west coast of Ecuador. Owing to its remote location, it is home to several endemic species (i.e., species not found anywhere else in the world). There are about a dozen central and minor islands in the archipelago. Daphne Major is small, nondescript, and remote even by the standards of Galápagos (Darwin did not even see it) and measures barely a square kilometer in area.
格兰特夫妇于1973年登陆达芙妮主岛开展研究项目。如第五章所述，达尔文曾于1835年到访加拉帕戈斯群岛。该群岛位于厄瓜多尔西海岸近一千公里处，因地理位置偏远而孕育了多种特有物种（即全球仅存于此地的物种）。这片群岛由约十二座主岛与小岛构成。 即便以加拉帕戈斯群岛的标准衡量，达芙妮主岛也显得渺小平凡且位置偏远（达尔文当年甚至未曾发现该岛），其面积仅约一平方公里。
29.
The next time I feel like complaining about the firmness of the mattress or the softness of the pillows in a hotel room, I hope I remember what the Grants endured on Daphne Major for six months every year for forty consecutive years. Before the Grants and their research staff made it their home, no humans had lived there. How could they? There is only one way to enter the island, and that is the base of a cliff since the island has no shoreline.
下次若再想抱怨酒店床垫太硬或枕头太软时，但愿我能记得格兰特夫妇在达芙妮主岛上的经历——他们连续四十年间每年都要在那里艰苦生活六个月。在这对夫妇及其研究团队进驻之前，这座岛屿从未有人类居住。毕竟怎么可能呢？整座岛没有海岸线，唯一的入口位于悬崖底部。
30.
To set up their camp every year, here is what the Grants had to do. They had to abandon their boat as they neared the island and approach the cliff base at low tide in a rowboat called a panga. A single black ledge filled with barnacles was their only landing point; it’s the size of a large exercise mat. They needed to leap onto this mat while the panga swayed wildly in the ocean waves. From here, they had to walk up the cliff on hard, wet rocks until they arrived at another ledge that they called the Landing. Then they formed a human chain and passed up tents, poles, water, food, and everything else they would need for the next six months. Daphne Major has no water or food. They could pitch their tents only at one spot, which was the size of a table and on the rim of a volcano. They had to lug all their supplies there from the base of the cliff. They cooked their meals in a small cave protected from the harsh sun by a sheet of cloth. Once settled, they would spend the next six months measuring the body size, beak size, and beak shape of every finch on the island. They would also extract blood to do a genetic analysis.
每年搭建营地时，格兰特夫妇需要完成以下步骤：在靠近岛屿时弃船，乘一艘名为"潘加"的划艇趁着退潮抵达崖底。布满藤壶的黑色岩脊是他们唯一的登陆点，面积仅如大型健身垫。他们必须在潘加艇随浪剧烈摇晃时跳上这块"垫子"。登陆后需踩着坚硬湿滑的岩石攀爬悬崖，直至抵达被称作"登陆台"的另一处岩架。随后组成人链传送帐篷、支架、饮用水、食物及未来半年所需的一切物资。达芙妮主岛既无淡水也无食物，他们只能在火山口边缘一处餐桌大小的平地扎营，所有物资都必须从崖底人工搬运上来。他们在用布帘遮阳的小洞穴里烹煮食物。安顿妥当后，夫妇二人将用六个月时间测量岛上每只雀鸟的体型、喙部尺寸及形状，并采集血液样本进行基因分析。
31.
Why would anyone in their right mind spend time on such an island? Because it offered many advantages that few other places could. Daphne Major was so far removed from the other islands that there was only a minimal chance that the finches or other island species could escape or that new ones could arrive. It was a perfect petri dish for evolutionary studies because it would not get “contaminated.” Also, the Grants realized that the Galápagos were a gold mine for testing and refining Darwin’s theory. The Galápagos Islands undergo extreme climate swings—from severe drought to copious rain—thereby providing ample opportunity for natural selection. Last, Daphne Major was small enough to track every bird that lives there over many years, which they did. From 1973 to 2012, the Grants tagged about twenty thousand birds over eight generations, and they tracked almost every copulating pair and their offspring.
为何心智健全之人会耗费光阴驻留这样一座岛屿？只因它具备罕有其匹的独特优势。达芙妮岛与其他岛屿隔绝甚远，雀鸟等物种既难逃离，外来物种亦难侵入。这座岛屿犹如未经污染的完美培养皿，堪称进化研究的理想实验室。更关键的是，格兰特夫妇意识到加拉帕戈斯群岛正是验证与完善达尔文理论的绝佳场所——群岛气候在极度干旱与丰沛降雨间剧烈波动，为自然选择提供了充沛的观测条件。最后，达芙妮岛的微型生态恰好允许研究者持续追踪岛上每只鸟类，这正是他们的实践：1973至2012年间，格兰特夫妇为八代近两万只雀鸟标记编号，几乎记录了每对交配个体及其后代的完整谱系。
32.
In the first four years of their study—from 1973 to 1976—the island received average or excess rainfall. But in 1977, after a brief spell of rain in January, Daphne Major suffered a severe drought. Almost all the greenery disappeared from the island, and the only plants that survived the drought were cactus bushes. The finches fed on seeds of all sizes. The small and medium-sized seeds disappeared very soon, and the finches were then left with only large and hard seeds. But only the finches with larger beaks could break open these larger seeds; finches with small and medium-sized beaks could not open these seeds and so starved and perished. The island was inhabited mainly by two species. At the start of 1977, there were about 1,200 Geospiza fortis and 280 Geospiza scandens. By the end of the year, only 180 fortis and 110 scandens were left. About 85 percent of the fortis and 60 percent of the scandens had succumbed to the drought.
在他们研究的前四年——从1973年到1976年——岛上降雨量正常或偏多。但1977年1月短暂降雨后，达芙妮主岛遭遇严重干旱。岛上几乎所有绿色植物都消失了，唯一熬过旱灾的是仙人掌丛。雀鸟们以各种大小的种子为食。中小型种子很快消耗殆尽，雀鸟只剩下又大又硬的种子可吃。但只有喙较大的雀鸟才能啄开这些大种子；中小型喙的雀鸟无法破开这些种子，最终饿死。 岛上主要栖息着两种雀鸟。1977年初约有1200只中地雀和280只仙人掌地雀。到年底时，仅剩180只中地雀和110只仙人掌地雀。约85%的中地雀和60%的仙人掌地雀未能熬过这场旱灾。
33.
The Grants’ data showed that the surviving fortis were, on average, 5 to 6 percent larger than the ones that had died. The average beak length of the survivors was 11.07 millimeters compared to 10.68 millimeters for the species before the drought; the average beak depth had increased from 9.42 millimeters to 9.96 millimeters. These differences may appear small to us, but they can tilt the balance between life and death in the wild. For example, a fortis with a beak length of 11 millimeters can crack a seed from a plant called a caltrop, but an individual with a beak length of 10.5 millimeters will not even attempt to do so. Just half a millimeter can make all the difference.
格兰特夫妇的数据显示，幸存的中地雀体型平均比死亡个体大5%至6%。幸存者的平均喙长为11.07毫米，而干旱前该物种的平均喙长为10.68毫米；平均喙深则从9.42毫米增长到9.96毫米。这些差异对人类而言或许微不足道，但在自然界却足以决定生死。例如，喙长11毫米的中地雀能咬开蒺藜植物的种子，而喙长10.5毫米的个体甚至不会尝试——半毫米之差便有天壤之别。
34.
The intensity of natural selection the Grants observed wasn’t limited just to beak length and depth. At the start of 1977, there were 600 male and 600 female fortis. By the end of the drought, more than 150 males and only a few females were alive. The males are typically larger than the females by about 5 percent, so the males were more likely to survive. There were now six males to every female. Natural selection can occur in a single generation, but evolution takes place only over many generations. Given the dramatic change in the beak size and sex ratio of the finches, natural selection had happened. But what about evolution? The Grants had to wait one more year to measure the beak size and shape of the offspring of the drought survivors of 1977. But then, they saw evolution in action, too—the new generation was 4 to 5 percent larger than the population before the onset of the drought.
格兰特夫妇观察到的自然选择强度不仅限于鸟喙的长度和深度。1977年初，岛上共有600只中地雀雄鸟和600只雌鸟。旱灾结束后，仅有150余只雄鸟和寥寥数只雌鸟存活。雄鸟体型通常比雌鸟大5%左右，因此存活概率更高——此时雄雌比例已达六比一。 自然选择可能在一代之内发生，但进化却需要历经多代。地雀鸟喙尺寸与性别比例的剧变，已印证了自然选择的存在。但进化呢？格兰特夫妇又等待了一年，测量1977年旱灾幸存者后代的喙部尺寸与形状。这一次，他们见证了进化的实况：新生代地雀的体型比旱灾前的群体增大了4%至5%。
35.
They soon realized that their observation of 1977 was not a fluke. In 1983, there was a very heavy rainfall because of El Niño, and the island became lush and green. Green vines covered even the cactus bushes. This changed vegetation had a significant impact two years later when drought struck again. This time, tiny seeds were abundant (produced by the vines of 1983), and large seeds became rare. As a result, the finches with large beaks found it hard to pick up the seeds, and a large proportion of the survivors of this drought were the small-beaked finches. Their offspring, as a result, also had smaller beaks. Natural selection and evolution were evident again, except that, unlike in 1977, the beaks had evolved to become smaller.
他们很快意识到1977年的观察并非偶然。1983年厄尔尼诺现象带来强降雨，岛屿变得郁郁葱葱，绿色藤蔓甚至覆盖了仙人掌丛。这种植被变化在两年后干旱再度来袭时产生了重大影响——此时遍地都是微小种子（由1983年的藤蔓产生），而大颗粒种子变得稀少。结果，大嘴地雀难以啄食种子，这场干旱中的幸存者多为小嘴地雀。因此它们的后代鸟喙也更为短小。自然选择和进化再次显现，只是与1977年相反，这次鸟喙朝着更小的方向演化。
36.
Thus, in less than a decade, the finches’ beak size increased and then decreased because of natural selection. When observed over a decade, beak size may not have appeared to change that much, but it had fluctuated significantly in the intervening period owing to the two extreme climate events. The Grants published the results of their long-term study of the evolution of the two species of finches—fortis and scandens—in Science in April 2002 under the title “Unpredictable Evolution in a 30-Year Study of Darwin’s Finches.”8 Before starting their research, they had hypothesized that the body traits of the finches would vary within a narrow band over the period of the study. But as they say at the beginning of the article, “The data do not support the expectation of no change.”
因此，在不到十年的时间里，由于自然选择的作用，雀鸟的喙部尺寸先增大后缩小。若以十年为观察周期，喙部尺寸的变化或许看似微小，但在这期间受两次极端气候事件影响，其实已发生过显著波动。 2002年4月，格兰特夫妇在《科学》期刊发表了题为《达尔文雀三十年研究揭示不可预测的进化》的长期研究成果，研究对象包括中地雀和仙人掌地雀两个物种。研究伊始，他们曾假设这些雀鸟的体型特征会在研究期间保持小幅波动。但正如文章开篇所述："数据并不支持性状不变的预期。"
37.
If you examine the graphs of the body size, beak size, and beak shape of fortis and scandens in the Grants’ article, you will notice the inverse correlation between the measurement period and the pace of evolution pointed out by Kurtén in brown bears. Thus, while the body size of fortis fluctuated a lot between the mid-1980s and the early 2000s, the overall change over those two decades was small. Similarly, the annual variation of the beak size of scandens appeared to be much greater than the variation from the mid-1970s until the early 2000s. The beak shape of fortis was practically the same when measured in the mid-1980s and the early 2000s, but its annual change was much more significant. There is a lovely fractal-like property to this phenomenon. It does not seem to matter if the measurement period is thousands of years (bears) or just a few decades (finches). The pace of evolution speeds up over shorter periods and slows down over more extended periods.
若细览格兰特论文中地雀（fortis与scandens）的体型、喙部尺寸及喙形的数据图表，便会注意到库尔滕在棕熊研究中指出的测量时长与进化速度的反向关联。以地雀为例：1980年代中期至2000年代初，其体型虽波动剧烈，但二十年间的整体变化微乎其微；同样，scandens喙部的年度变异幅度，远大于1970年代中期至2000年代初的累积变异；而fortis的喙形在1980年代中期与2000年代初几乎毫无二致，但年度变化却极为显著。 这种现象具有美妙的分形特性——无论测量时段跨越数千年（如棕熊）或仅数十年（如地雀），进化速率皆呈现短期加速、长期放缓的规律。
38.
But so what? Why should long-term investors care about Kurtén’s bears and the Grants’ finches? The Grants and Kurtén Dictate When We Buy and Sell My observations are nowhere as scientific as the Grants’ or Kurtén’s. However, I know that the daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly rate of change in exceptional businesses appears much greater than the rate of change measured over years and decades. This realization has helped me formulate an investing principle that I call the Grant–Kurtén principle of investing (GKPI). It goes as follows: When we find high-quality businesses that do not fundamentally alter their character over the long term, we should exploit the inevitable short-term fluctuations in their businesses for buying and not selling.
但这又如何？长期投资者为何要在乎库尔滕研究的熊与格兰特夫妇研究的雀鸟？ 格兰特与库尔滕指导买卖时机 我的观察远不及格兰特夫妇或库尔滕那般科学。但我发现，卓越企业的日度、周度、月度及季度变化速率，远高于以年或十年为单位的衡量尺度。 这一认知让我总结出名为"格兰特-库尔滕投资法则"(GKPI)的原则，其核心是： 当我们发现长期本质未变的高质量企业时，应当利用其业务中必然出现的短期波动来买入——而非卖出。
39.
GKPI requires us to own high-quality businesses that do not fundamentally alter their character over the long term. “High-quality businesses” is a loaded term and can mean different things to different investors. If you have come this far, I assume you know our definition. For us, the critical character traits of a high-quality business are stellar operating and financial track records, a stable industry, a high governance standard, a defensible moat, increasing market share, and low business and financial risk. How do we use GKPI for buying? By exploiting short-term fluctuations.
GKPI要求我们持有那些长期不会发生根本性性质变化的高质量企业。"高质量企业"是个内涵丰富的术语，对不同投资者可能意味着不同标准。既然您已读到这里，想必了解我们的定义。对我们而言，高质量企业的关键特质包括：卓越的经营与财务记录、稳定的行业属性、高治理标准、可防御的护城河、不断提升的市场份额，以及较低的经营与财务风险。 我们如何运用GKPI进行买入操作？通过把握短期波动机会。
40.
We are highly demanding. We want a company to be run by an honest management team, and show solid operating and financial track records over many years. It needs to stay ahead of the competition and be debt free, and we also want it to keep taking calculated risks while not unduly burdening the business. And, as if all these demands were not enough, we dare to insist on a fair price for these rare gems! How is this even possible? The market should almost never offer us an attractive price for such a business, and it doesn’t. However, on rare occasions, investors who don’t subscribe to GKPI succumb to the pressure of temporary macro, industry, or company issues, and we can then swoop in to buy a piece of an exceptional company. It doesn’t—and shouldn’t—happen often, but when it does, we go all in.
我们向来苛刻。我们要求企业必须由诚信的管理团队执掌，并展现出多年稳健的经营与财务记录。它需要保持行业领先地位且无负债缠身，同时要在不拖累企业的前提下持续承担可控风险。更甚者，我们竟敢为这些稀世珍宝坚持要求合理价格！这怎么可能？市场几乎从不让我们以诱人价格购得此类企业——事实也确实如此。 然而在极少数情况下，当那些不认同GKPI理念的投资者被宏观环境、行业或公司的短期困境压垮时，我们便能趁机买入杰出企业的部分股权。这种情况既不会——也不该——频繁发生，可一旦出现，我们必定全力以赴。
41.
Take WNS, one of our largest investments to date. It is one of India’s leading business process outsourcing (BPO) companies and is listed on the New York Stock Exchange. It was set up in 1996 as a captive back office by British Airways and started serving third-party clients in 1999. In 2002, Warburg Pincus acquired a controlling stake in WNS. (I wasn’t involved in the deal.) Although British Airways contributed to about 90 percent of WNS’s revenues at the time of acquisition by Warburg Pincus in 2002, its revenue share had fallen to less than 10 percent by early 2008. The management at WNS had done a stellar job of diversifying away from British Airways and the travel industry. By outsourcing back-office functions like finance and accounting, mortgage processing, and customer analytics, WNS’s clients typically get higher-quality service (e.g., faster turnaround times, lower error rates) at a much lower cost.
以我们迄今为止最大规模的投资之一WNS为例。这家印度领先的业务流程外包（BPO）企业在纽约证券交易所上市，1996年作为英国航空旗下自营后台部门成立，1999年开始为第三方客户提供服务。2002年，华平投资收购了WNS的控股权（我未参与该交易）。尽管被收购时英国航空贡献了WNS约90%的收入，但到2008年初这一比例已降至不足10%。WNS管理层在摆脱对英国航空及旅游行业依赖的转型中表现卓越。通过将财务核算、抵押贷款处理、客户数据分析等后台职能外包，WNS客户通常能以更低成本获得更优质服务（如更快的周转速度、更低的差错率）。
42.
In January 2008, the stock price of WNS fell sharply from a previous high of $35 to $13. Three factors seemed to have contributed to this steep correction. First, one of its large mortgage lender clients, First Magnus, declared bankruptcy in late 2007 owing to the ongoing subprime mortgage crisis in the United States. First Magnus had contributed to about 5 percent of WNS’s revenue, so the management lowered its revenue guidance for the next year. Second, the management indicated that it may lose Aviva, a large insurance client that was contributing to about 8 percent of WNS’s revenue, by May 2008 if Aviva were to acquire the WNS facility servicing it. And third, the appreciating rupee had the analysts worried since most of WNS’s delivery presence was in India. (A stronger rupee could make WNS less competitive and squeeze margins.)
2008年1月，WNS股价从35美元的历史高位暴跌至13美元。三大因素似乎共同导致了这次剧烈回调：首先，受美国次贷危机影响，其重要抵押贷款客户First Magnus于2007年底宣告破产，该客户约占WNS总收入的5%，管理层因此调低了次年收入预期；其次，管理层表示若保险公司Aviva收购为其服务的WNS设施，可能在2008年5月失去这个贡献8%收入的大客户；最后，卢比持续升值令分析师担忧——由于WNS主要交付中心位于印度，卢比走强可能削弱其竞争力并压缩利润空间。
43.
The question for us was this: Was this a temporary blip, or was it a more permanent decline of achievable revenue and profit growth? We decided it was the former. The loss of First Magnus wasn’t WNS’s fault, and even the potential loss of Aviva would be due to Aviva’s change of strategy, not because WNS wasn’t serving it well. Also, we didn’t believe WNS’s long-term competitive advantage would be adversely affected by exchange rate fluctuations. WNS’s annualized revenue growth of 64 percent from 2004 to 2007 was proof that it offered high-quality back-office processing services to its clients at competitive prices.

So here are the answers to the two questions I posed at the start of this section. First, “long term” is genuinely long term: at least five decades, maybe more. And second, new businesses do replace old ones, but at a far slower rate than we think. This brings us back to the lesson from the Grants and Kurtén. There is no point wasting time, energy, and brainpower on worrying about day-to-day upheavals in high-quality businesses. They can be highly resilient over the long term. So why sell? ✳✳✳
以下是对本节开头两个问题的回答。首先，"长期"是真正意义上的长期：至少五十年，或许更久。其次，新企业确实会取代旧企业，但速度远比我们想象的缓慢。 这让我们回归到格兰特和库尔腾的启示。在优质企业日常波动上耗费时间、精力和脑力毫无意义，它们往往具备穿越周期的强大韧性。 那么，为何要卖出呢？ ✳✳✳

Evolutionary theory has taught me that . . . . . . we can reimagine investing by embracing the tenet that the long-term character of high-quality businesses remains unaffected by short-term fluctuations in the economy, the industry, and even the business. 1. Contrary to the expectations of Darwinism, evolution can be faster when measured over shorter periods and slower over longer periods. Björn Kurtén demonstrated this phenomenon in the evolution of the teeth of European brown bears. 2. High-quality businesses, too, seem to undergo many changes when measured over days or weeks or months but are much more stable when the period of measurement is years or decades. 3. Empirical data from the longevity of Fortune 500 businesses demonstrate the long-term resilience of exceptional businesses. About 40 to 45 percent of the Fortune 500 businesses of 1955 continued to be successful for the next sixty years.
进化论教会我…… ……我们可以重新构想投资方式，秉持一个核心原则：优质企业的长期特质不会因经济、行业乃至企业自身的短期波动而改变。 1. 与达尔文主义的预期相反，以短期衡量时进化可能更快，而以长期衡量时则更慢。比约恩·库尔滕通过欧洲棕熊牙齿的进化现象证实了这一点。 2. 优质企业同样如此：以日、周或月为单位衡量时看似变化频繁，但以年或十年为单位衡量时则表现出极强的稳定性。 3. 《财富》500强企业存续时间的实证数据表明，卓越企业具有长期韧性。1955年上榜的企业中，约40%至45%在此后六十年间持续保持成功。
